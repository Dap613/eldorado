# Eldorado — Project Context

## Client
Eldorado is a 10-acre park near the Great Ocean Road offering boat & caravan storage,
affordable long-term living, and holiday escapes.

## Channels in Scope
- **Google Ads** — search and display campaigns
- **Website** — WordPress CMS
- **Email** — campaigns and automations (provider TBC)
- **SEO** — on-page and off-page optimisation

## Data Sources (current)
- `data/eldorado-tp6.html` — exported performance report

## Planned Integrations
- Email provider (MCP / export)
- WordPress site (MCP / API)
- Google Ads portal (MCP / export)
- Google Analytics (GA4)

## Goals
Drive bookings and enquiries across all three revenue streams:
1. Boat & caravan storage
2. Long-term living / residential sites
3. Holiday park bookings
